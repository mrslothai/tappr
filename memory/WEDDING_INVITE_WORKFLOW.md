## How to Make REAL Wedding Videos (Step-by-Step)

### **Tools You Need:**
1. **Runway ML** (runwayml.com) - Photo animation
2. **CapCut** (Free) - Video editing
3. **Canva** (Optional) - Templates

## **WORKFLOW:**

### **Step 1: Gather Assets (5 mins)**
- 5-10 couple photos (high quality)
- Wedding details:
  - Names: Rahul & Priya
  - Date: 15th February 2025
  - Venue: The Grand Ballroom, Hotel Taj
  - City: Mumbai

### **Step 2: Animate Photos in Runway (10 mins)**

1. Go to runwayml.com
2. Click "Image to Video"
3. Upload couple photo
4. Use "Motion Brush" - paint over faces for subtle movement
5. Settings:
   - Motion: 3-5 (subtle)
   - Duration: 4 seconds
   - Export: 1080p
6. Repeat for 3-4 photos

**Pro Tip:** Keep movement slow - just breathing/living effect

---

### **Step 3: Edit in CapCut (15 mins)**

**Timeline Structure:**

```
00:00 - 00:05  |  Black screen + "Save the Date" text
                |  Soft fade in, romantic music starts

00:05 - 00:10  |  Couple photo 1 (animated from Runway)
                |  Text overlay: "Together Forever"

00:10 - 00:15  |  Couple photo 2 (animated)
                |  Text: "Rahul & Priya"

00:15 - 00:20  |  Couple photo 3 (animated)
                |  Text: "Invite you to celebrate"

00:20 - 00:30  |  Mandala/decorative background
                |  Wedding details appear one by one:
                   - Date
                   - Venue
                   - City

00:30 - 00:40  |  Final couple photo
                |  "You're Invited"
                |  "Bless us with your presence"

00:40 - 00:45  |  End screen with contact/RSVP
                |  Music fades out
```

**CapCut Features to Use:**
- Transitions: "Fade", "Dissolve", "Zoom"
- Text: "Elegant" or "Wedding" font styles
- Effects: "Glow", "Sparkle"
- Color grading: Warm tones (orange/gold)

---

### **Step 4: Add Music (2 mins)**

**Free Music Options:**
1. **YouTube Audio Library** (copyright free)
2. **CapCut music library**
3. **Uppbeat.io** (free tier)

**Best Songs for Indian Weddings:**
- Instrumental version of "Kesariya"
- "Raataan Lambiyan" (slow)
- "Tum Hi Ho" (Aashiqui 2)
- "Mere Liye Tum Kaafi Ho"

**Sync Tips:**
- Add text on beat drops
- Photo changes on music transitions

---

### **Step 5: Export & Deliver (3 mins)**

**CapCut Export Settings:**
- Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Format: MP4
- Quality: High

**Delivery:**
- Upload to Google Drive
- Share link with client
- Or send via WhatsApp (compress if needed)
