## Getting Started

### 1. Open SmartPass
Visit the SmartPass website in your browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).

### 2. Enable Notifications (Optional but Recommended)
When you first open the app, you'll see a blue banner asking to enable notifications.

**Why enable?** You'll get automatic reminders:
- 2 hours before your flight
- 30 minutes before boarding
- When boarding starts

**How to enable:**
1. Click the "Enable" button
2. Click "Allow" in your browser's popup
3. Click "Test" to verify it works

### 3. Add Your First Boarding Pass

#### Step 1: Get a Photo
- Take a photo of your printed boarding pass, OR
- Take a screenshot of your digital boarding pass from airline app/email

#### Step 2: Upload
1. Click the upload area (or drag and drop your image)
2. Select your boarding pass image
3. Wait 3-5 seconds for processing

#### Step 3: Verify Details
The app will automatically extract:
- ‚úàÔ∏è Flight number (e.g., AA123)
- üë§ Your name
- üí∫ Seat number (e.g., 12A)
- üö™ Gate number

- üìÖ Flight date

Your boarding pass is now saved!

## Troubleshooting

### "Could not extract valid boarding pass information"
- Try a clearer photo
- Ensure the boarding pass has visible text
- Some airline formats may not be supported yet

### Notifications Not Showing Up
- Check browser notification permissions
- Verify boarding time is in the future
- Some browsers block notifications in private mode

### App Not Working Offline
- Load the app once with internet first
- Check service worker is registered (DevTools)
- Try hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)

### Lost All My Passes
- Check you're using the same browser
- Clearing browser data removes passes
- Consider taking screenshots as backup

## FAQs

**Q: Is SmartPass free?**
A: Yes! Completely free, no ads, no premium tier.

**Q: Does it work with all airlines?**
A: It works with most standard IATA boarding passes. If yours doesn't work, try taking a clearer photo.

**Q: Can I use it on multiple devices?**
A: Currently, data is per-device only. No cloud sync in MVP version.

**Q: What if my flight gets delayed?**
A: You'll need to update the boarding pass manually or wait for future versions with real-time flight tracking.

**Q: Is my boarding pass data secure?**
A: Yes! Nothing is uploaded. Everything stays on your device. We can't see your data because we never receive it.

**Q: Can I export to Apple Wallet or Google Pay?**
A: Not yet, but this is planned for a future version!

**Q: Does it work in airplane mode?**
A: Yes! Once you've loaded the app and saved your passes, everything works offline.

**Q: How much storage does it use?**
A: Very little. Each boarding pass is ~50-100KB. You can save hundreds.
