# Instagram Reel Ideas - Rajesh's Content Strategy

## REEL #1: "How I'm Making AI Do My Job (For Free)"

**Status:** ✅ Ready to shoot

**Why it works:**
- Money + AI tools = your audience's top interests
- Specific, actionable tools (not vague advice)
- High-value feeling without being salesy

**Script (50 seconds):**
- Hook: "I'm literally earning money while I sleep using 3 free AI tools."
- Tool #1: ChatGPT for freelancing ($500-1000/month)
- Tool #2: Midjourney/Leonardo for stock photos (Etsy/Shutterstock passive income)
- Tool #3: 11labs Voiceover (audiobooks, YouTube, podcasts)
- CTA: "Which tool you trying first? Comment below. I'll send a free mini-course."

**Timeline:** 30 min shoot, 15 min edit

**Engagement prediction:** High (money angle always performs)

## REEL #2: "Stop Learning AI, Start Building With It"

**Status:** ✅ Ready to shoot (RECOMMENDED FIRST)

**Why it works:**
- Contrarian hook (engagement gold)
- Builds authority + positions you as a builder
- High comment potential (people disagree = algorithm boost)
- Targets frustrated learners in your audience

**Script (45 seconds):**
- Hook (Provocative): "Stop. You don't need to learn ChatGPT prompting. You need to build something."
- Problem: "Everyone's taking 10 courses on AI. 90% never build anything."
- Solution: "I picked ONE problem. Used ChatGPT. Built in 3 days. Sold for $500."
- Your action: "Pick a problem you face daily. Build it this week."
- CTA: "What problem will you solve? Drop it below. Might feature your idea!"

**Timeline:** 20 min shoot (you on camera + screen recording), 12 min edit

**Why post this FIRST:**
- Builds authority + follower investment
- Creates community (people drop ideas = engagement)
- Makes followers MORE interested in Reel #1 later (monetization comes after authority)

**Engagement prediction:** Very high (contrarian + community-building)

---

## RECOMMENDED POSTING SEQUENCE:

1. **This week:** Post Reel #2 "Stop Learning AI, Start Building With It"
   - Builds your authority as a builder
   - Creates community engagement (CTA asks for ideas)
   
2. **2 days later:** Post Reel #1 "How I'm Making AI Do My Job"
   - Converts engaged followers into revenue mindset
   - They already know you're credible from Reel #2
